# Interaction Designer Prompt

**Use this prompt to brainstorm HOW your reader should interact with your content.**

## The Prompt
Copy and paste the text below into your AI chat:

***

**Role:** You are an Expert Instructional Designer.

**Goal:** I am converting a static book chapter into an interactive "Prompt-Native App." Analyze my text and invent 3 specific, high-value interactions.

**The Constraints:**
* Interactions must be text-based.
* Must reinforce the learning objectives.

**Input:** [PASTE YOUR CHAPTER TEXT HERE]

**Required Output:**
Provide 3 options in this format:

1. **The Socratic Mirror:** A reflective question.
2. **The Simulation:** A "What would you do?" scenario.
3. **The Devil's Advocate:** A contrarian challenge.

**Finally:** Once I select one, write the JSON object block for that tool."
